## Top-line themes

1. **Two end-to-end TOTP gaps make 2FA functionally optional.** `signInAction` redirects authed users to `/app` even when they have a TOTP factor enrolled. Middleware does not enforce a "2FA pending" state. The only thing keeping the user out of the app is the existence of the step-up cookie — which is never required for `/app` access. PRD §7.1 says "TOTP mandatory at signup." This build does not enforce it. (F-TRC-03, F-TRC-04.)
2. **Dual audit-event writers with conflicting contracts.** `packages/db/src/audit.ts` throws on failure; `packages/logger/src/audit.ts` is the correct never-throw retry-via-BullMQ implementation. Every Server Action imports the throwing one and wraps in try/catch. Pick one and converge. (F-TRC-01.)
3. **Skip-link broken on 4 pages.** `<main id="main">` missing on `app/app/onboarding/totp`, `app/app/onboarding/backup-codes`, `app/app/settings/security`, `app/sign-in/totp`. Root layout's skip-link sends users into the void. (F-HALO-01.)
4. **Banned word "platform" on the public landing page** (F-PRF-01 / F-CAN-01). Brand-locked term per PRD §13.3.
5. **WebAuthn options endpoints are not rate-limited.** Any authed caller can mint unlimited challenges. (F-TRC-02.)
6. **Privacy policy publishes with placeholder DPO and a 404 subprocessors link.** GDPR Art. 13(1)(b) requires DPO contact at publication. (F-CMP-01, F-CMP-02.)
7. **5 Cipher/2FA cookies set but undisclosed** in `/legal/cookies` (F-CMP-07).

## Passes worth noting

- **Cookie consent flow is APG-correct.** Banner `role="region"` ✓ (not dialog). Preferences as Dialog ✓ with focus-trap, ESC, opener-return. Pre-checked defaults are OFF ✓ (Recital 32 / Planet49). Necessary cookies don't gate ✓.
- **HIPAA refusal contract** present and properly framed in Terms (F-PRF-04, F-CAN-10).
- **Crypto primitives are solid.** TOTP verify normalised + constant-time. Backup-code matching `timingSafeEqual` and accumulates without short-circuit. Step-up tokens HMAC-bound to purpose with TTL.
- **`import "server-only"` correctly gates** `apps/web/lib/supabase/service.ts` and `apps/web/lib/twofa/cookie.ts`.
- **`user_2fa_factors` migration** has both shape constraints, partial unique indices, RLS with force, security-definer audit triggers with locked search_path.
- **OpenTelemetry attribute hygiene** in `packages/telemetry/src/llm.ts` — only numeric metadata, never raw prompts.
- **Logger redaction surface** (`packages/logger/src/logger.ts`) covers `authorization`, `cookie`, `set-cookie`, `req.body.password`, `req.body.token`, `job.data.access_token`, BullMQ variants.
