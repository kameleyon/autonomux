### F-Compass-02 · Minor · Sidebar nav order is alphabetic-ish; PRD doesn't lock an order — current order is operationally sensible

### F-Compass-03 · Major · Dashboard cards do NOT chunk by purpose — Hick's law concern for a polymath operator

11 cards in a single auto-fit grid (`(authed)/page.tsx:14-81`). For a high-cognitive-load polymath operator (PRD §2), grouping these would speed scan:

- **Runtime ops:** Tenants · Queue · Health · Activity log
- **Money:** Costs · Billing
- **Trust:** Audit log · Compliance · Integrations health
- **Levers:** Feature flags · Support

Fix: split SECTIONS into 4 groups with `<h2>` headings and sub-grids each. (Also called out in Optic-06.) Defer if scope is strictly scaffold-only.
